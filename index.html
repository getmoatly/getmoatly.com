The Framework

Four questions. Every stock.
One framework.

Most investing apps give you data and leave you to figure out what it means. Moatly gives you a framework — four questions every serious investor must answer before committing a dollar, quantified and scored for every stock you search.

Meaning

Do you truly understand this business? Moatly quantifies your fit using your Circle of Competence and a five-question Conviction Check — producing a Meaning score that's unique to you. Same stock, two investors, completely different scores. Because the best investment is the one you'll hold without flinching.

MOAT

Does this business have a durable competitive advantage? Moatly scores it using the Big Five growth metrics — revenue, earnings, equity, operating cash, and free cash flow — compounded across 1, 3, 5, 7, and 10 year windows. No analyst opinions. Just the math of whether this business is widening the gap.

Is leadership genuinely owner-oriented? Moatly scores ROE, ROIC, ROA, debt discipline, and the proprietary MER Score — measuring how effectively management deploys your capital. Great businesses are routinely destroyed by poor stewards. Know which kind you're dealing with.
